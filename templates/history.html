This club started its journey officially form Fall-2014 addressing Zaforullah Momtaz, Assistant Professor,
CSE, UAP, as it’s convener and Monowar Ul Islam Sajib (Batch-30) as it’s acting President. It was then
named “Software and Hardware Club”. 

In the next semester Spring-2015 acting president Monowar Ul Islam Sajib (Batch-30) was assigned as President. 
He Conducted “UAP CSE Intra Department Carnival” under the convening of Zaforullah Momtaz. 

In the next semester Fall-2015 Sifat Shaharear (Batch-31) was assigned 
as this club reforms its committee after every semester (6 months). 

In the following semester Spring-2016 M.R.Ferdous Kiron (Batch-32) was assigned as president. He Conducted 
“CSE UAP Digital Universe Exposition – 2016” under the convening of Zaforullah Momtaz. 

In the following semester, Fall-2016 Abir Hasan (Batch-33) was selected the president of this club and 
Dr. MD.Rashedul Islam, Associate Professor, CSE, UAP was made the convener of the club. In this semester
a national fest called “Inter University Hardware and Software Carnival - 2017” where around 100 university
of Bangladesh participated. 

After that semester, In Spring-2017 Muntasher Morshed (Batch-34) came in as president 
under the convening of  Dr.MD. Rashedul Islam. 

In Fall-2017, Gazi MD. Hasnat Zahan, Lecturer, CSE, UAP came in as convener of the club and Faruq Ahmed Masum
(Batch-35) came in as president and he organised two programs, one is intra university program which is 
called “Digital Universe Exposition, Season-2”, where contestants participated from different departments
of UAP and another one is Inter University program called “Inter University Software and Hardware Carnival-2018”
over 150 universities of Bangladesh participated here on different segments.

In Fall-2018, Dr.MD. Rajibul Islam, Assistant Professor, CSE, UAP came in as convener of the club and 
Khandakar Atique Shahrier(Batch-37) came in as president and he organized intra university program which
is called “Digital Universe Exposition, Season-3”. 

Md. Shoyeb Hossain (Batch-39) came in as president and he organised intra university program which is called 
“3rd UAP CSE Inter University Software and Hardware Carnival-2020” and Cyber Security Challenge Workshop. 
In Spring-20 MD. Jahidul Hasan Mojumder(Batch-40) came in as president and lead this club ahead. 

In Fall-20 Jahir Ibna Rafiq, Assistant Professor, CSE, UAP came in as convener of the club and Armanur Rahman Pranto(Batch-41)
came in as president and he organized  Online Digital Universe Exposition-2021 (Season-4). First ever online event
in this club. over 100 of UAP CSE students registered for this event. And also create a workshop on Graphics design
where over 120 student joined this workshop. Still now this club is working for the welfare and improvement in software
 and hardware skills of students.

Club Motto

We believe that today's tech world’s place will be slowed down without the knowledge of software and hardware. Our motto is to improve and patronize our student’s software and hardware skills. We are already done with Android and Application Development Session, Web Application and Development, Hardware and its Application Development. We try our best to help the students which are interested in exploring the software and hardware universe. We are looking forward to arranging some workshops and classes for the students for their betterment. We are also up to starting a “Club Laboratory” where we will be providing the students some equipment which they can use in different projects of software and hardware. We want to help our students to be the mastermind in the software and hardware sector who will represent and enlighten our university.
